Our History

Unfolding Our History, One Bake at a Time

  1. 2015

    The first loaf

    In 2015—with more determination than capital, and a simple promise to earn an honest living through quality bakes—our founder pulled the very first loaf from a modest kitchen in Bulathsinhala. Through every barrier, he served the neighbourhood with warmth and consistency.

  2. Growing

    Word of mouth

    As word spread through the community — not through advertising, but through honest taste and genuine care — the little bakery began to grow. New products joined the shelves: cream buns, seeni sambol rolls, celebration cakes, and seasonal treats that became household favourites across generations.

  3. Expanding

    Across Sri Lanka

    Penetrating all barriers which stood before them, Peoples Bakers kept expanding while maintaining the impeccable essence of their products. New outlets opened in Nugegoda, Kandy, Galle, and beyond. The bakery that started before dawn at a single counter had become a trusted name across Sri Lanka — loved for its consistency, its quality, and its heart.

  4. Today

    Digital & delivery

    Today, with 150+ outlets island-wide, an online ordering platform, and partnerships with Uber Eats and PickMe Food, Peoples Bakers continues to grow — while holding firmly to the belief that every bake must carry the same love as the very first loaf.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Become the leading figure in the industry as the food manufacturer, by producing, distributing and marketing best quality products for our customers to satisfy their needs and wants.

Our Mission

Achieving the success in the business sector, with higher customer satisfaction and building up a strong relationship with all the business related parties through a productive process by offering a product of fresh, healthy, high standard quality with less production cost and minimum waste.
